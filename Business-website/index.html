How To Choose a Template

If you would like to choose one of our template sites, just click on one of the templates on the right of this page to see a bigger version of it. Once you choose a template, you can choose how you want your site to look.

Here are the items you can choose to style your site to your liking:

  • Font Styles
  • Font Colors
  • Font Sizes
  • Navigation Bar Design
  • Pictures Layout
  • Pictures Borders
  • Pictures Effects
  • Any videos you may want on your page
  • Any music files you may want on your page
  • Do you want your music to autostart when page is clicked on
  • Links to your Social Media
  • Links to other sites of your choosing
  • Background Colors
  • Background Images
